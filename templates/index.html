Esti-Mate: A Web-based Construction Quantity and Cost Calculator

A web-based construction calculator calibrated from Philippine construction standards. Esti-Mage delivers fast, accurate quantity and cost estimates through a simple, user-friendly interface, with location-based pricing for more reliable project planning.

About Us

Welcome to Esti-Mate, a web-based construction quantity and cost calculator designed to simplify and enhance the planning process for construction projects. Our goal is to provide accurate, efficient, and user-friendly tools that help professionals, students, and project planners make informed decisions with confidence.

Our Mission
At Esti-Mate, we aim to streamline the estimation process by reducing manual calculations and minimizing errors. We believe that reliable data and intuitive technology can significantly improve productivity and project outcomes in the construction industry.
Our Vision
We envision Esti-Mate becoming a trusted digital companion in the construction industry, empowering users with accurate tools and innovative solutions for smarter project planning.
What we do
Esti-Mate allows users to:
  • Estimate project costs quickly and efficiently
  • Improve planning and budgeting accuracy
  • Save time through automation and a user-friendly interface
  • Calculate construction quantities with precision
